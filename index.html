    ABOUT ME

    Hello! My name is Ma. Nicole B. Morales. I was born on October 30, 2005, in Davao City but raised in Baganga, Davao Oriental. Currently, I live in Davao City to pursue my studies and experience living independently. I’m a Computer Science student at the University of the Immaculate Conception. My favorite color is a shade close to brown, much like the color of coffee, as I’m a coffee enthusiast. I also love traveling and dream of visiting Paris someday. Ice cream is my comfort food because it gives me a sense of calm and peace. I’m passionate about exploring new places and constantly learning, both in life and in my academic journey. I really love being alone most of the time as it gives me peace and makes me calm. When I am alone, I can think peacefully. However, sometimes I love being with my friends, as they also give me happiness and are my source of energy. Choosing Computer Science was the most unexpected decision I ever made because it wasn't part of my plan. It sparked my curiosity, and I decided to take the risk. I don’t think it’s a good idea to always follow our curiosity and let our impulsive thoughts win,
